NUTRIENT DEFICIENCY

IRON DEFICIENCY

CAUSES

Not eating enough iron rich foods, Coeliac disease, blood loss which is any sort of bleeding causes iron deficiency.

SYMPTOMS

Extreme fatigue, pale skin, chest pain, poor appetite, inflammation.

FOOD TO BE TAKEN

  • Dark green leafy vegetables.
  • Dried fruits, such as raisins and apricots.
  • Seafood.
  • Beans, Peas.
  • Red meat, pork and poultry.

CALCIUM DEFICIENCY

CAUSES

Poor calcium intake in food, medications that decrease calcium absorption, hormonal changes, especially in women.

SYMPTOMS

Fatigue, muscle pains, spasms, abnormal heart rythm, numbness, weak and brittle nails.

FOOD TO BE TAKEN

  • Milk, tofu, almonds.
  • Broccoli
  • Green leafy vegetables, beans.
  • Salmon, tuna, sesame seeds, oranges.

IODINE DEFICIENCY

CAUSES

Not consuming iodized salt, not consuming fish and mutton, having a vegetarian diet causes iodine deficiency.

SYMPTOMS

Weight gain, fatigue, hair loss, dry skin, swelling in the neck, feeling cold and slow heart rate.

FOOD TO BE TAKEN

  • Eggs, tuna.
  • Seaweed, yogurt.
  • Iodized salt.
  • Baked cod, Shrimp.
  • Cranberries, fruits.

VITAMIN A DEFICIENCY

CAUSES

Less intake of animal food products and vegetables, diseases affecting pancreas cause vitamin A deficiency.

SYMPTOMS

Dry skin and dry eyes, night blindness, delayed growth, throat and chest infections, poor wound healing.

FOOD TO BE TAKEN

  • Dairy products like milk, cheese, butter.
  • Eggs, fish, liver.
  • Carrots, pumpkin, sweet potatoes.
  • Green leafy vegetables, mango, watermelon, papaya.

VITAMIN C DEFICIENCY

CAUSES

A restrictive diet due to health conditions like weak digestive system, allergies, old age, Hyperthyroidism, Pregnancy.

SYMPTOMS

Poor immunity, dry and wrinkled skin, Scurvy, muscle and joint pain, weak bones, bleeding gums and teeth loss.

FOOD TO BE TAKEN

  • Citrus fruits like orange, lemon, guava and strawberries.
  • papaya and kiwi fruit.
  • Tomatoes and broccoli.
  • spinach, cabbage, green and red bell peppers.

VITAMIN D DEFICIENCY

CAUSES

Less exposure to sunlight, having overweight or obesity, having chronic kidney disease, liver disease, or hyperparathyroidism, being older.

SYMPTOMS

Bone and back pain, hair loss, fatigue, muscle pain, weight gain and anxiety.

FOOD TO BE TAKEN

  • Fatty fish and egg yolks.
  • milk and fruit juices.
  • cereals and yogurt.
  • beef liver.
  • cod liver oil, mushroom, soy milk.

VITAMIN K DEFICIENCY

CAUSES

Taking excessive antibiotics, have a condition that causes the body to not absorb fat properly (fat malabsorption), have a diet that is extremely lacking in vitamin K.

SYMPTOMS

Delay in blood clotting, over bleeding, gets small blood clots underneath the nails, bleeding in the skin, nose, the gastrointestinal tract, or other areas.

FOOD TO BE TAKEN

  • Green leafy vegetables like spinach, broccoli.
  • Green beans, peas, cabbage and avocado.
  • Pork liver, chicken liver and beef kidneys.
  • Cheese, milk, egg yolks and butter.

VITAMIN B1 DEFICIENCY

CAUSES

Drinking alcohol, older age, AIDS, increased blood sugar levels, high dose use of medicines causes vitamin B1 deficiency.

SYMPTOMS

Loss of appetite, difficulty in breathing, paralysis, fits , difficulty in walking, nausea.

FOOD TO BE TAKEN

  • Cereals, bread, pasta, and rice.
  • Pork, fish, meat and eggs.
  • Legumes, including black beans and soybeans.
  • Whole grains, nuts and seeds.
  • Yogurt and corn.

VITAMIN B2 DEFICIENCY

CAUSES

Chronic disorders like diarrhoea, liver problems and alcoholism, malabsorption of food, Blood filtration procedures, such as haemodialysis .

SYMPTOMS

Mouth cracks at corners, mouth ulcers, red and sore tongue, scaly skin, itchy and watery eyes, photophobia.

FOOD TO BE TAKEN

  • Low-fat milk and eggs.
  • Green vegetables, including broccoli and spinach.
  • Fortified cereals, grains, and bread.
  • organ meats, including kidney and liver.

VITAMIN B3 DEFICIENCY

CAUSES

Chronic alcoholism and poor dietary intake of vitamin B3 are causes of this deficiency.

SYMPTOMS

Dermatitis, diarrhoea, loss of memory, indigestion, vomiting, depression, fatigue, scaly skin, rash that resembles sunburn and may darken in sunlight.

FOOD TO BE TAKEN

  • Meat products, particularly beef liver.
  • Poultry, such as turkey and chicken and eggs.
  • Fish, such as salmon and tuna.
  • Nuts and legumes, oil seeds.

VITAMIN B6 DEFICIENCY

CAUSES

Excessive loss of vitamin B6 during hemodialysis, Chronic kidney disease, autoimmune disorders, malabsorption disorders and excees use of drugs.

SYMPTOMS

Hyper irritability, nausea, fits, skin rashes, decreased immunity, anaemia, Inflamed, red, and sore tongue (glossitis).

FOOD TO BE TAKEN

  • Chickpeas, beef liver, roasted chicken breast.
  • Potatoes, bananas.
  • Turkey meat, ground beef.
  • Breakfast Cereals (fortified).

VITAMIN B12 DEFICIENCY

CAUSES

Following strict vegan diet, pernicious anaemia, gastric surgeries, intestinal problems and excessive alcohol consumption.

SYMPTOMS

Extreme weakness and fatigue, glossitis and mouth ulcers, breathlessness and dizziness, Loss of appetite and weight loss, Some loss in sense of touch, walking and vision problems.

FOOD TO BE TAKEN

  • Beef, liver, chicken and fish.
  • Eggs, Broccoli, spinach, asparagus and lima beans.
  • Fortified foods, such as breakfast cereals.
  • Milk, cheese and yogurt.
